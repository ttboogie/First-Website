What is the Living Wage?


Living wage is the minimum income that a person needs to make to fulfill their basic needs. This includes the price of food, housing, and other necessities including clothing. The living wage applies to the cost of living in any location. It is based on the number of children, number of working adults, the number of adults not working, as well as the average prices of rent in the area.
