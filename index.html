Why is it important?


Living wage laws are important because it sets a wage for workers that they should be able to buy food, pay for housing, and have other necessities in life. Without a living wage, people cannot live comfortably or securely. However, even with a living wage, it is different for everyone depending on their family situation - meaning the living wage should be raised.
